Desnooded turkeys on a factory farm

Our Executive Director and Research Director's salaries and the organization’s administrative expenses are covered through 2017 by a grant from the Effective Altruism Foundation. With another $23,000 we can hire a researcher, and with an additional $6,000 we can run some of the RCTs and/or surveys in our research agenda, primarily a US poll that assesses people's current moral circle and attitudes towards animal farming. We're aiming to reach that total $29,000 by the end of July. Donations in excess of this will provide us with more financial security so our Executive Director Kelly can spend less time fundraising and more time conducting research and creating content. Since we just launched, we’d like to focus on our research through December so we can establish our proof of concept before making a major fundraising push at the end of the year.

Raised: $26,592
Research hire: $23,000
July goal: $29,000

We are in the process of applying for our US 501(c)(3) tax exemption, and in the meantime, the Centre for Effective Altruism, a registered US 501(c)(3), has generously offered to act as our fiscal sponsor. You can make a tax-deductible donation to Sentience Institute by donating through a special fund the Centre for Effective Altruism created for us on Effective Altruism Funds. UK donors can also claim Gift Aid for their contribution.

The link below will direct you to the Sentience Institute fund and will show a fund allocation of 100%. Click the "Looks good!" button to donate by credit card, debit card, bank transfer, wire, or check.

Please note that because donations go through EA Funds, we can see the total amount donated but are not notified immediately of donor names or individual amounts. We deeply appreciate your support and would love to be in touch with you sooner rather than later, so feel free to get in contact with kelly@sentienceinstitute.org to let us know you supported us! We'll have a more formal donation process in the future.
